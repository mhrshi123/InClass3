Global Language: English

Effective Ways to learn English

English is the most commonly studied second language in the world. There have never been more study materials at your fingertips, articles promising secret hacks, or online communities to support you. But if you need to learn English quickly, which is the easiest path to results? n this guide, we have pulled together a step-by-step rundown of the techniques that have proven to deliver progress fast, why they work and some resources that can help you follow them.

It has never been easier to find reading materials in English, or quicker to look things up on Google Translate if you get frustrated. If you are not quite ready to tackle the complete works of Shakespeare, try: Browsing celebrity gossip on easy-reading websites like Buzzfeed, Mashable or Upworthy Reading a book you love in your native language in the English translation Switching your search browser to show English language results by default Following more American/British/Australian public figures on Twitter and Facebook.
